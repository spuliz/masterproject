Welcome to Schedio, your AI-assisted fashion design tool

Schedio is a web application to help fashion designers create finished design images of bags and garments in few seconds.
Thanks to Schedio AI system, designers can test different combinations of textures applied on their sketches.

How does it work?

  1. Upload an image of a fashion sketch



  2. Upload a texture image



  3. Click CREATE and wait for the image to be created
